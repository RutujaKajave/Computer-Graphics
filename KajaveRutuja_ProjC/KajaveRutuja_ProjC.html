Functionalities :
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

1. Press the arrow keys to move around the field.

2. Press 'W', 'S', 'A', 'D' keys to change perspective view of the field

3. Press 'I', 'J', 'K', 'L' keys to change the position of the lights

4. Press 'Space' key to turn the lamp light on or off

5. Press 'Enter' key to turn the headlight on or off

6. Press 'M' key to change the material/colour of the sphere
